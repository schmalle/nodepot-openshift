Openshift
---------

Openshift is a great cloud solution from Red Hat. It offers free hosting for NodeJS.

1. Create an account at openshift

2. Create your own application

rhc app create <your name> nodejs-0.10 --from-code=http://github.com/schmalle/nodepot.git

3. Add redis to your application

rhc add-cartridge http://cartreflect-claytondev.rhcloud.com/reflect?github=smarterclayton/openshift-redis-cart


https://github.com/smarterclayton/openshift-redis-cart
